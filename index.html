Module Lattice
Operates over R_q = Z_q[X]/(X²⁵⁶+1) with q = 8,380,417. Security reduces to Module-LWE and Module-SIS problems, believed resistant to quantum computers.
🔄
Rejection Sampling
Signer samples mask y, computes w = Ay, checks if z = y + cs₁ leaks the secret. Aborts and retries when infinity norm exceeds bound γ₁ − β.
⚡
NTT Acceleration
Polynomial multiplication uses the Number Theoretic Transform in O(n log n) over Z_q. Precomputed ζ-tables enable fast convolutions over degree-256 rings.
📋
NIST FIPS 204
Standardized in 2024 as ML-DSA. Dilithium3 targets ~128-bit post-quantum security, designed as the primary replacement for ECDSA and RSA.
